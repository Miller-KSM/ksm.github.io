April, 2021

Contoso Retail Analysis

The Contoso dataset is obtained from Microsoft and being restored to local SSMS. Denormalization are performed in SSMS to create Star Schema data model and imported to Power BI through VIEWs, remaining transformation/modeling are performed using DAX & Power Query in Power BI. Cardinalities are set to Many:1 and single filter direction.
The report contains 3 separate pages and are fully interactive, feel free to click around.
